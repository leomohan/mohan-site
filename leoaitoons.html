About the Series

AI Satire for the Enterprise

leoAIToons is a weekly satirical cartoon series exploring the absurdity of enterprise AI — the vendor promises, governance theatre, procurement dynamics, and the gap between AI hype and operational reality.

Created by Mohan Krishnamurthy, each cartoon is a one-panel commentary on the state of AI in organisations across the Middle East and beyond. Leo the Lion is our guide — part mascot, part sceptic, always watching.
