About Me

Techie by Day; Storyteller by Night — a journey spanning Tamil Nadu, Bahrain, and the UAE.

💼 LinkedIn ▶ YouTube ✍ Blog

The Story

From Tamil Nadu to the UAE

I'm Mohan Krishnamurthy — known professionally as Leo Mohan — a cybersecurity strategist, prolific author, and lifelong learner based in Dubai, UAE.

My journey began in a small town in Tamil Nadu, India, where a deep curiosity about technology and an even deeper love of stories took root early. Over 30+ years, that curiosity has taken me across India, Bahrain, and the UAE — and into the boardrooms of some of the region's most forward-thinking organisations.

Today I serve as General Manager of Evanssion FZCO, a cybersecurity and AI Value-Added Distributor operating across MEA, and Novaala ME FZCO, an AI strategy and solutions company.

The Author

But the boardroom is only half the story. By night — and on weekends, during flights, and in quiet cafés — I write. With over 263 published books spanning technology, fiction, business, humor, philosophy, spirituality, and Tamil-language works, I've built one of the most prolific self-publishing catalogs in the MEA technology community.

My early books were co-authored with major publisher Syngress/Elsevier (2006–2009). Since then, I've published independently through Amazon KDP, Draft2Digital, Lulu, and Apple Books — reaching readers in over 50 countries.

Beyond the Desk

I maintain an active LinkedIn presence with 10,000+ followers, publishing a thought leadership article every Wednesday. My YouTube channel has grown to 2,500+ subscribers, and I host the leoAIToons satirical cartoon series.

I hold 33+ vendor certifications across the cybersecurity and cloud landscape. When not writing or strategising, I'm travelling — camera in hand, collecting new stories and perspectives from around the world.
